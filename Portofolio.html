Adolphe's Biography

Adolphe Kalisa is a student at Southern New Hampshire University through the Kepler Kigali Program. He has been studying here since June 2017 and is expected to graduate in June 2020. Adolphe has been a member and leader of different clubs and organizations. He currently works in the Kepler Hackathon Program which works on different programming projects in Kimironko sector.

Adolphe has been a secretary for The East African Community club in Lycee de Kigali from 2015 to 2016 and a journalist of Media Club Lycee de Kigali. Adolphe has a High School Diploma from Lycee de Kigali, and is currently enrolled in the Associate of Arts in General Studies with a Specialization in Business. He is now currently working on a project with the Kepler Hackathon team to create a website for The Innovation Club and Hackers' Hub. Adolphe Kalisa currently lives in Kigali, Rwanda.
